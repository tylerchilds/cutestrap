Grid

Basic grid made using flexbox. Direct children of .grid are rows and direct children of rows are columns.

<main class="grid">
  <article>
    <section class="[modifier class]">
      Cell 1
    </section>
    <div class="grid">
      <aside>
        <p class="grid [modifier class]">
          Cell 2.1
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Cell 2.2</li>
        </ul>
      </aside>
    </div>
  </article>
</main>
